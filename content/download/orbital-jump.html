Orbital Jump

Welcome to Orbital Jump

Orbital Jump takes gaming to a new level of realism, delivering an authentic experience of space navigation. Built in Unity, this game challenges you to master the complex mechanics of gravitational forces as an astronaut armed with nothing but a jetpack.

This isn’t just a game—it’s a simulation. Orbital Jump mimics the behavior of gravity across vast distances, with forces that weaken the farther you drift from a planet. Each move demands precision and careful planning, and some levels require you to calculate your entire trajectory before launching. What seems like a straightforward journey quickly becomes a test of physics, timing, and spatial awareness.

Are you ready to explore the most realistic portrayal of interplanetary navigation in gaming?

Game Highlights

  • True-to-Life Physics: Experience gravity as it works in space—dynamic, ever-changing, and deeply realistic.
  • Skill-Based Gameplay: Master the art of precision, timing, and calculated movement.
  • Strategic Puzzles: Plan ahead and predict your path to conquer increasingly intricate levels.

  2. Extract the files from the archive.

Your interstellar mission awaits—prepare to defy gravity like never before!
